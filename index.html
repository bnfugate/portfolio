Etiquette in the Workplace

Etiquette in the workplace is something incredibly vital in a work environment and it was something I learned quickly at my summer job. Etiquette in the workplace are unwritten rules that people are expected to follow, and if those rules are not followed it can create tensions. One example of etiquette in the workplace is not stealing someone else’s food out of the fridge. This may seem like something small, but people get angry and take it personally when their food is taken. It usually results in passive aggressive behaviors or people trying to get payback, which is not something needed in a workplace.
